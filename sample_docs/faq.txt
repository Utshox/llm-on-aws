Q: What industries does Pro-Tech serve?
A: Water and wastewater treatment, manufacturing, food and beverage, and energy.

Q: Which PLC brands do you work with?
A: Primarily Allen-Bradley (Rockwell) and Siemens, plus other platforms on request.

Q: Do you offer remote monitoring?
A: Yes. Pro-Tech provides remote SCADA monitoring and support so issues can be
diagnosed without an on-site visit.

Q: How does a typical project start?
A: With a site assessment to understand the existing equipment and the goal,
then a design proposal and quote.

Q: Do you support legacy system upgrades?
A: Yes. Many projects involve modernizing older control systems while keeping
the process running with minimal downtime.

Q: How can I get a quote?
A: Contact the team with a short description of the site and the problem, and
an engineer will follow up to scope the work.
