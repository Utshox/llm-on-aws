Pro-Tech Systems Group is a B2B industrial automation company. It designs and
integrates SCADA systems, PLC programming, and control panels for water
treatment, manufacturing, and energy clients. The company serves clients across
the United States and provides remote monitoring, system integration, and
ongoing technical support.

Core services include SCADA development, HMI design, PLC configuration,
industrial networking, and bulk water management systems. The team works with
Allen-Bradley, Siemens, and other major automation platforms.

Customers contact Pro-Tech for new control-system builds, upgrades to legacy
systems, and emergency support. Typical projects start with a site assessment,
followed by design, build, factory acceptance testing, installation, and
commissioning.
